Be An Advocate

Poster Design

In the midst of a 1000-piece jigsaw puzzle, I was inspired to develop a series of visual stories using toys to tell messages of conservation. I believe it can be challenging to inspire non-animal-lovers to feel passionate about issues of animal welfare, so I thought that toys would be a more grounding concept that all people could relate to. I hope everyone can find these images somewhat uncomfortable.

Tools Used: Adobe Photoshop & InDesign
