Text Design

In my Type and Image class at the Rhode Island School of Design, my professor asked us to think about a paragraph of text as an image as opposed to just a body of content. He gave us a few different nouns that he wanted us to show using text. We were not allowed to use any text distortion effects, but instead manipulate lines and letters to convey meaning.

Tools Used: Adobe InDesign
